Given risk and chosen leverage, this calculates the required margin size (USD) so that if SL is hit, loss ≤ risk-per-trade.

Fixed Margin Mode

Given risk and the margin you're willing to allocate, this calculates the maximum leverage to keep loss ≤ risk-per-trade.

Terms:
  • Risk per trade: USD amount you would lose if stop-loss is hit. Calculated as Wallet × Risk%.
  • Margin: USD collateral required for the position on the exchange (isolated/cross depending on your settings).
  • Leverage: Position size / margin — how many times your margin is amplified.
